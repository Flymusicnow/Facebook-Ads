# Ad Creative Tests for The Clarity Shop

## Creative A: Clarity and Self-Respect

**Hook:** Mixed signals are still an answer.

**Visual:** Premium workbook cover on a clean beige background. Minimal styling, soft shadows, calm editorial layout.

**Angle:** Clarity and self-respect.

**Image direction:** Show the digital workbook cover as the hero object. Use a warm beige or cream background with generous spacing. Keep the design polished, simple, and premium.

**Image text:** Mixed signals are still an answer.

**Primary text:** If someone keeps you confused, you do not need to chase harder. You need a clear way to understand what is happening and choose yourself with calm self-respect.

**Headline:** The Situationship Exit Strategy

**CTA:** Shop Now

**Target emotion:** Relief from confusion and permission to stop over-explaining someone else's inconsistency.

**Winning metric:** CTR over 2% signals the hook and product-led visual are strong enough to inspect product page and checkout performance.

## Creative B: Phone Anxiety and Waiting for a Reply

**Hook:** Still checking if they care?

**Visual:** Realistic woman holding a phone in soft natural light. Emotional but premium, not overly dramatic. The expression should feel quiet, reflective, and familiar.

**Angle:** Phone anxiety / waiting for a reply.

**Image direction:** Use a lifestyle-style image with warm light, clean composition, and a calm neutral palette. The phone should be visible, but the emotion should carry the image.

**Image text:** Still checking if they care?

**Primary text:** If their replies decide your mood, it may be time to step back and get clear. This 7-day workbook helps you sort the confusion without chasing, begging, or losing yourself.

**Headline:** Stop Waiting. Get Clear.

**CTA:** Shop Now

**Target emotion:** Recognition of the exhausting loop of checking the phone and waiting for reassurance.

**Winning metric:** CTR over 2% with strong thumb-stop performance; if CTR lands between 0.8% and 1.5%, test new image variations with the same hook.

## Creative C: Emotional Truth and Self-Worth

**Hook:** Confusion is not connection.

**Visual:** Minimal text image on a cream background with refined serif typography. No clutter, no loud colors, no over-designed elements.

**Angle:** Emotional truth / self-worth.

**Image direction:** Create a premium quote-card style image using cream, ivory, or warm beige. Use one strong line of text, centered or slightly offset, with a high-end editorial feel.

**Image text:** Confusion is not connection.

**Primary text:** You can care about someone and still choose peace. The Situationship Exit Strategy helps you understand the pattern, stop spiraling, and make a clear next move.

**Headline:** Choose Clarity Over Confusion

**CTA:** Shop Now

**Target emotion:** Calm conviction and self-worth without anger or drama.

**Winning metric:** CTR over 2% proves the emotional-truth hook is strong; under 0.5% means the visual or hook should be killed.
