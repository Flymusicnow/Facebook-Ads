# The Clarity Shop Content Style Rules

Codex must use this file before creating captions, ads copy, Shopify copy, product descriptions, social schedules, campaign summaries, or creative ideas for The Clarity Shop.

## Tone

The tone should be:

* simple
* warm
* direct
* emotionally clear
* gentle
* premium
* easy to understand

## Avoid

Avoid:

* therapy jargon
* spiritual cliches
* fake scarcity
* aggressive sales copy
* shame-based messaging
* overexplaining

## Best content structure

Use this structure:

1. Hook
2. Pain recognition
3. Soft solution
4. CTA

## Good phrases

* "Tired of overthinking the same situation?"
* "Get clear on your next step."
* "Stop guessing what mixed signals mean."
* "Rebuild your self-worth without spiraling."
* "For when your mind needs calm, not more confusion."
* "Real-life clarity in a digital guidebook."
* "Instant digital download. No physical product shipped."
