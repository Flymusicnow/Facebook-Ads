# The Clarity Shop Brand Guide

Codex must use this file as the default brand reference before creating or editing anything related to The Clarity Shop.

## Brand name

The Clarity Shop

## Brand promise

Beautiful digital guidebooks for real-life clarity.

## Brand feeling

The brand should feel:

* calm
* premium
* warm
* clear
* feminine
* safe
* emotionally intelligent
* practical

## Visual style

Use:

* cream
* beige
* soft gold
* sage green
* dusty pink
* lavender
* soft blue
* warm peach
* thin gold lines
* soft shadows
* rounded cards
* elegant serif headlines
* clean sans-serif body text

## Avoid

Avoid:

* neon colors
* harsh red
* cheap urgency
* cluttered ecommerce layout
* aggressive discount language
* loud sales graphics
* generic dropshipping style

## What the brand sells

The Clarity Shop sells:

* clarity
* self-respect
* emotional relief
* decision power
* safer relationships
* less overthinking
* inner stability
